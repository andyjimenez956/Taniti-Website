Explore Taniti

Taniti offers a wide variety of activities for visitors who want to relax on the beach, explore the rainforest, learn about local history, or enjoy a fun night out. Use this page to discover what you can do during your stay.

Outdoor Adventures

Beaches

Relax on Taniti’s white sandy beaches around Yellow Leaf Bay or explore the more rugged rocky shoreline. Many visitors spend most of their time enjoying the sun and swimming.

Rainforest Hikes

Guided hikes take you through lush tropical rainforest, where you can learn about local plants and wildlife while enjoying scenic views of the island.

Volcano Visits

Visit Taniti’s active volcano on a guided tour. Learn about the island’s geology, take photos from designated viewpoints, and experience one of Taniti’s most memorable sights.

Water Activities

Snorkeling

Enjoy clear waters and colorful marine life with beginner-friendly snorkeling options. Local guides can help you find the best spots around the island.

Chartered Fishing Tours

Book a chartered fishing tour and spend a day on the water with experienced local crews. This is a great way to experience Taniti’s fishing heritage.

Boat Tours

Take a boat tour around the island or across Yellow Leaf Bay to see coastal cliffs, small coves, and beautiful sunset views.

City & Culture

History Museum

Learn about Taniti’s history, culture, and traditions at the local history museum, a great stop for visitors of all ages.

Art Galleries

Visit art galleries that feature local artists and island-inspired pieces. Many galleries are located in and around Merriton Landing.

Helicopter Rides

Experience Taniti from above with a helicopter tour offering aerial views of the rainforest, beaches, and volcano.

Merriton Landing & Nightlife

Pubs & Microbrewery

Enjoy local drinks at several pubs and a microbrewery in Merriton Landing, a popular area for evening activities.

Dance Club & Arcade

Spend the night dancing at a new club or visit the arcade for games and family-friendly fun.

Movies & Bowling

Catch a movie or go bowling for a relaxed evening activity close to restaurants and shops.

Coming Soon

Nine-Hole Golf Course

A nine-hole golf course is under development and is expected to open soon, adding another way to enjoy your time outdoors on Taniti.
